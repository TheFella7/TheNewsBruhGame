📌 Printing Press Tycoon Basics

  • Tap the big "PRINT EDITION" button to earn New (currency). Each tap gives +1 New × multiplier.
  • Buy Assets (Paperboy, Editor, etc.) in the right column. They produce New per second (CPS) automatically.
  • Build up the BREAKING NEWS BOOST by tapping – fill the red bar to activate a 5× multiplier for 15 seconds.
  • Level up by earning XP from taps and from passive income (every 10 New earned = 1 XP).
  • Prestige (Buy Bruh) in the left column. Resets everything but gives a permanent +100% (×1) production bonus per Bruh owned.
  • Chat with other players in the Press Room – use @name to tag someone.
  • Your progress is auto-saved locally and to the cloud. Use the MANUAL SAVE button anytime.
